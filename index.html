✧*̥˚ About Laufey*̥˚✧

Laufey (pronounced lāy-vāy) is a Chinese-Icelandic musician whose goal is to bring jazz and classical music to her generation through a more accessible medium. “There are very few examples of artists who are onstage with an orchestra one day and making TikToks the next,” she notes dryly.

Laufey's nostalgic sound—enchanting her listeners with her deep-hearted lyrics. "Bewitched," her new album, won a Grammy Award for Best Traditional Pop Vocal Album. Her current and future successes will stun our generation: revitalizing the feel of romance, heartbreak, and growth.
